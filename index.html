    Overview

    Toolbox Overview¶

    The KGA Toolbox is a powerful, all-in-one geoprocessing solution designed to accelerate and enhance GIS workflows in land management, cadastral processing, and spatial data quality assurance. Built for ArcGIS Pro, the toolbox brings together a wide range of essential tools—spanning data creation, data management, topology validation, spatial analysis, and automated quality checks—into one streamlined and easy-to-use package.

    With dedicated modules for coordinate system conversion, parcel data cleaning, overlap detection, topology rule enforcement, and buffer and clip operations, the KGA Toolbox enables GIS professionals to work faster, maintain cleaner datasets, and produce accurate results with confidence. Whether you are preparing datasets for analysis, validating land parcels, or generating map-ready layers, the toolbox provides a structured workflow that reduces manual effort and minimizes errors. Designed for teams and organizations that rely on consistent, repeatable geoprocessing tasks, the KGA Toolbox delivers efficiency, reliability, and professional-grade spatial data processing—making it an ideal addition to any ArcGIS Pro-based environment.

    Key Capabilities¶

    KGA Toolbox come with various and flexible capability to ensure the effeciency of GIS workflow in Land Parcel data management. Below are key capabilities of the toolbox.

    Coordinate System¶

    • Accurate conversion from local Indian to WGS84 UTM Converter
    • Ensures positional integrity for all downstream processing

    Data Creation¶

    • Generate boundary points, central points, and parcel-based linework
    • Create LMAP layers and convert polygons to map-series-ready point-line structures
    • Automate foundation layers for mapping and analysis

    Data Management¶

    • Batch add fields across multiple feature classes
    • Copy and organize parcel datasets into geodatabases
    • Count features and export tables to structured CSVs with a single click

    Geoprocessing Tools¶

    • Multi-feature clip operations
    • Multi-ring buffers for impact analysis
    • Percentage coverage calculations
    • Parcel trimming along road edges for cleaner geometry

    Quality Control¶

    • Detect duplicate parcel numbers and clean attribute fields
    • Validate field formats and identify overlapping parcels
    • Ensure data completeness, consistency, and reliability

    Spatial Analysis¶

    • Compute point-to-point direction and distance
    • Generate spot heights and support terrain-related workflows

    Topology Validation¶

    • Add and enforce topology rules
    • Detect overlapping boundaries and incorrect point placements
    • Perform comprehensive topology checks on parcel datasets

    Why Choose KGA Toolbox?¶

    • All-in-one solution for cadastral and spatial data workflows
    • Reduces manual effort and standardizes processes across your team
    • Improves data accuracy with built-in quality control and topology checks
    • Accelerates project turnaround with automated, repeatable tools
    • Designed for GIS professionals who manage complex land information datasets
