## __Maintenance, Upgrade & Support Policy__
### License Scope

Each __KGA Toolbox license__ is node-locked and tied to the specific ```hardware device(s)``` registered at the time of activation. The license may not be transferred, shared, or resold without prior written approval from [Khmer GRS Academy (KGA)](https://khmergrs.com){:target="blank"}.

### Maintenance & Updates

All valid licenses include:

* Bug fixes and performance improvements
* Minor feature enhancements
* Compatibility updates for supported ArcGIS Pro versions
* Maintenance updates are provided at no additional cost during the active license period.

### Upgrades

Minor upgrades (e.g., feature refinements, new tools within the same major version) are included free of charge.

* Major version upgrades may require an upgrade fee or license renewal, depending on the scope of changes.
* License holders will be notified in advance of any major version release and applicable upgrade terms.

### Technical Support

Licensed users are entitled to:

* Email and messaging-based technical support
* Assistance with installation, activation, and configuration
* Guidance on correct tool usage and known limitations

Support does not cover:

* Issues caused by unsupported software versions
* Hardware or operating system failures

### Device Replacement Policy

In the event of hardware failure or device replacement, a license reset may be granted once, subject to verification and approval by __KGA__.

### License Duration

Licenses are issued on a __lifetime basis__, as specified at the time of purchase.

### Policy Amendments

[Khmer GRS Academy](https://khmergrs.com){:target="blank"} reserves the right to amend pricing, maintenance, upgrade, and support policies with prior notice. Continued use of __KGA Toolbox__ constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
